Answer:

  • Horizontal bars work better here because stadium names fit cleanly on the y-axis without overlapping, and it‚Äôs easier to compare bar lengths side-by-side.

Answer:

  • Position is more precise for comparing exact values, while color is better for grouping or emphasizing specific data points.
